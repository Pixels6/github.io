Studying hacks

  1. When studying, use colored pens on keywords to help trigger your memory
  2. Record yourself reading aloud your notes and listen to them
  3. Instead of trying to memorize it you can try explaining to them what you are studying and as you explain to them it will become clearer to you what
  4. When studying for a written test practice writing questions or answers that you think might be on the test to help with remembering
  5. Have time management to manage time wisely and avoid procrastinating
  6. When studying for an exam, Google “site.edu (subject) exam” to find exams on the same topic.
  7. Don’t forget to take breaks, 10 minutes for every 50 minutes spent studying.

  9. Make study groups with your buddies! Quiz each other and practice together!

Super-duper studying spaces

  1. Switch workspace when getting distracted
  2. On Ambient-Mixer.com, you can listen to the background ambience of fictional worlds, keeping you from getting distracted and studying peacefully.

Better time management

  1. Instead of putting work off work you can do later, do it now so that later you can have free time to do whatever
  2. Use an agenda to keep notes of important events (ex. tests, study times)
  3. Prioritize your time
  4. Plan ahead of time
  5. Colour coordinate your notes that can help you remember important points

Organization

  1. Use an agenda listing important due dates, assignments and homework
  2. Have a checklist or schedule at home with a list of things that must get done
  3. Colour coordinate your notes that can help you remember important points
  4. Highlight important notes

motivation

  1. Make attainable goals for yourself and reward yourself when you reach that goal, like more breaks or a treat!
